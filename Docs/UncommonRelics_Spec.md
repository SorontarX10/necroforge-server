# Uncommon Relics Spec (Necroforge)

Below is a ready specification for `RelicDefinition` and icon art briefs.

| ID | Display Name | Gameplay Description | Icon Art Brief |
|---|---|---|---|
| `uncommon_bone_ledger` | Bone Ledger | Gain +20% EXP from kills, but lose 8% Max Health. | A chained ledger bound in old leather and bone clasps, pages glowing with pale runes and tally marks carved like grave counts. |
| `uncommon_grim_oil` | Grim Oil | Gain +10% swing speed and +8 stamina regeneration. | A squat iron flask with black oil dripping over a nicked sword edge, ember reflections on tarnished metal. |
| `uncommon_sepulcher_lantern` | Sepulcher Lantern | Every 4th hit applies Wither for 4s: the target deals less damage and moves slower. If it dies, Wither jumps to the nearest enemy. | A funerary lantern with cracked green glass, corpse-moth silhouettes inside, hanging from a rusted hook. |
| `uncommon_warden_chain` | Warden's Chain | After a dodge, your next hit yanks the target toward you and roots it for a short duration. | A heavy iron chain looped around a hooked manacle, links etched with warding glyphs and dried grave mud. |
| `uncommon_funeral_standard` | Funeral Standard | Every 22s, plant a battle standard for 6s. While inside its aura, gain movement speed and damage reduction. | A torn black war banner on a barbed pole, skull finial, cloth edges glowing with faint blue ward-light. |
| `uncommon_crypt_judgement` | Crypt Judgement | Hitting the same target 3 times in sequence unleashes a Judgment Slash in a straight line through and behind that target. | A stone reliquary blade fragment suspended in a ring, split by a bright execution-line rune. |
| `uncommon_marrow_reservoir` | Marrow Reservoir | Convert 30% of overheal into barrier, up to 25% of Max Health. | A rib-cage vessel filled with luminous marrow fluid, bone valves and copper pins sealing the sides. |
| `uncommon_iron_vigil` | Iron Vigil | If you avoid damage for 7s, gain Vigil: +15% dodge chance until you are hit. | A battered kite shield with one watching eye sigil painted in chalk and blood, hairline cracks of blue light. |
| `uncommon_ossuary_page` | Ossuary Page | Every 24s, if no summon is active, call a Bone Scribe for 7s. The Scribe marks enemies, weakening their offense and amplifying your damage against them. | A floating parchment page stitched with vertebrae quills, lines writing themselves in ash ink. |
| `uncommon_penitent_quill` | Penitent Quill | The first hit you take every 15s is recorded. Land 5 hits in 4s to heal the recorded amount. | A long black quill pinned through a confession scroll, ink droplets shaped like tears and blood beads. |
| `uncommon_duelist_spur` | Duelist Spur | While exactly one enemy is nearby, gain +15% swing speed and +10% dodge chance. | A polished cavalry spur tied with a red duel ribbon, laid over crossed arming swords and chalk duel marks. |
| `uncommon_censer_of_rebuke` | Censer Of Rebuke | Every 30s, release a 5s smoke cloud around you. Enemies inside deal reduced damage while you gain steady health regeneration. | A swinging censer on a chain, venting dense silver smoke mixed with ember sparks and church-bell soot. |

## Style Guide (Icons)

- Palette: steel, bone, ash, old gold, dried blood red, rot green, ward blue.
- Material: hand-painted, worn medieval dark-fantasy with readable silhouettes at small size.
- Composition: one dominant relic object, minimal clutter, high contrast edge lighting.
- Lighting: torchlit and directional, no modern light sources, no sci-fi elements.
