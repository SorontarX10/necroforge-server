## Style Guide (Icons)

- Palette: obsidian black, old bone, blood red, ember orange, grave green, muted gold, and ash gray.
- Material: hand-painted medieval dark-fantasy, worn textures, strong silhouette readability at small size.
- Composition: one dominant relic object, centered focal rune, minimal background clutter.
- Lighting: torchlit and ritual directional light; no modern symbols, no sci-fi motifs.
