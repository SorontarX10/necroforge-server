## Style Guide (Icons)

- Palette: blackened steel, old bone, ash gray, ember orange, blood red, grave green, and muted gold.
- Material: hand-painted medieval dark-fantasy with worn surfaces and readable silhouettes at small size.
- Composition: one dominant relic object, clear focal rune, minimal clutter.
- Lighting: torchlit or ritual directional light, no modern motifs or technology.
