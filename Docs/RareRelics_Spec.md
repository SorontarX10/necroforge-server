## Style Guide (Icons)

- Palette: steel, bone, ash, blood red, grave green, ward blue, blackened brass.
- Material: hand-painted, worn medieval dark-fantasy with high readability at small icon size.
- Composition: one dominant relic object, minimal clutter, strong silhouette and rim-light.
- Lighting: torchlit, ritual and directional, with no modern motifs or technology.
